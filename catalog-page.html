How it was done. The <main> section containing the 18 color squares was set to display: grid, with 3 rows and 6 columns [grid-template: repeat(3, 1fr / repeat(6, 1fr);]. An @media query for 835px shrinks the layout for cell phones to 6 rows and 4 columns. In between, a second media query at 1000px transitions to 5 columns and 4 rows so all squares are visible down to cell phone resolution.
