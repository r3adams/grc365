Rich Adams is Professor Emeritus at Toronto Metropolitan (formerly Ryerson) University, School of Graphic Communications Management, in Toronto. As a part-time instructor at the University of Nevada at Las Vegas (UNLV), he has been excited to get back into the computer lab to teach his favorite topic, web design.

Beginning in Spring 2023, Rich taught GRC 365 Web and User Experience Design. In fall 2023 he taught a senior seminar class, GRC 420, Design & Media Studio III. The class covered current topics in graphic arts of interest to the students, including package design, user experience design, and product rebranding.

This site contains documents that Rich wrote for GRC 365 and 420 along with presentations for the Graphic Communications Education Association (GCEA) annual meeting.
